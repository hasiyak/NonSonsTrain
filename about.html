About NSTrainz

Connecting the nation, one scenic journey at a time

Our Mission

At NSTrainz, we believe that the journey is just as important as the destination. Our mission is to provide a seamless, secure, and modern ticket booking experience for both locals and tourists exploring the beautiful island of Sri Lanka.

We partner with Sri Lanka Railways to bring the century-old railway system into the digital age, ensuring that reserving your seat on iconic routes like the Colombo to Ella express is just a few clicks away.

Why Choose Us?

We are the leading online platform for railway reservations in Sri Lanka.
